## Production Process

### Video Creation Workflow

**Pre-Production:**
1. Topic selection (from list above)
2. Script outline (NOT word-for-word)
3. Gather examples/demos
4. Prep screen recordings

**Production:**
1. Record main footage (talking head or screen)
2. Record B-roll if needed
3. Screen recordings of demos
4. Keep it authentic, not overly produced

**Post-Production:**
1. Edit (keep tight, cut fluff)
2. Add chapters/timestamps
3. Thumbnail design
4. Title optimization
5. Description + links

**Tools:**
- Screen recording: OBS or ScreenFlow
- Editing: DaVinci Resolve (free) or Final Cut
- Thumbnails: Figma or Canva
- Hosting: YouTube

**Time per video:** ~4-6 hours (will improve with practice)

## Risk Mitigation

**Challenge:** Maintaining consistency while building product

**Solution:**
- Batch record 2-3 videos when possible
- Some videos ARE the product journey (easy content)
- Adjust schedule if needed (every 10 days vs weekly)
- Quality > Quantity

**Challenge:** Growing slowly

**Solution:**
- Focus on quality content
- Engage deeply with small audience
- Even 100 engaged subscribers = valuable for product launch
- Consistency matters more than virality

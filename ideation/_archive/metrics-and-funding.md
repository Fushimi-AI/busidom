# Business Metrics & Funding Support

## Core Concept

The system should help entrepreneurs track, understand, and optimize the business metrics that matter most - especially those critical for funding, investors, and stakeholders.

---

## Investor Readiness

### What Investors Ask

The system helps entrepreneurs prepare for common investor questions:

**Traction & Growth:**
- What's your revenue?
- What's your growth rate (MoM, YoY)?
- How many customers/users do you have?
- What's your customer acquisition trend?
- What's your retention rate?

**Unit Economics:**
- What's your Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)?
- What's your Lifetime Value (LTV)?
- What's your LTV:CAC ratio?
- What are your gross margins?
- What's your payback period?

**Market & Opportunity:**
- What's your Total Addressable Market (TAM)?
- What's your Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM)?
- What's your Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM)?
- What's your market share?
- How fast is the market growing?

**Business Model:**
- How do you make money?
- What's your pricing strategy?
- What are your revenue streams?
- What's your gross margin?
- What's your path to profitability?

**Team & Execution:**
- Why is your team uniquely positioned to win?
- What milestones have you hit?
- What's your burn rate?
- How long is your runway?
- What will you achieve with this funding?

**Competition:**
- Who are your competitors?
- What's your competitive advantage?
- Why will you win?
- What's your moat?

### Shark Tank Style Preparation

System helps prepare for rapid-fire questioning:
- Quick, confident answers to common questions
- Know your numbers cold
- Clear, compelling story
- Handle objections gracefully
- Practice pitch delivery

## Key Business Metrics Tracking

### Financial Metrics

**Revenue Metrics:**
- Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)
- Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR)
- Revenue Growth Rate
- Revenue by product/segment
- Average Revenue Per User (ARPU)

**Profitability Metrics:**
- Gross Profit & Gross Margin
- Operating Profit & Operating Margin
- Net Profit & Net Margin
- EBITDA
- Break-even point

**Cash Flow:**
- Cash burn rate
- Monthly cash flow
- Runway (months remaining)
- Cash reserves

### Customer Metrics

**Acquisition:**
- Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
- CAC by channel
- Conversion rates (funnel metrics)
- Lead-to-customer conversion
- Sales cycle length

**Retention & Engagement:**
- Churn rate (monthly, annual)
- Retention rate
- Net Revenue Retention (NRR)
- Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)
- Active users (DAU, WAU, MAU)
- Engagement metrics

**Satisfaction:**
- Net Promoter Score (NPS)
- Customer Satisfaction Score (CSAT)
- Customer effort score
- Product-market fit score

### Growth Metrics

- Month-over-Month (MoM) growth
- Year-over-Year (YoY) growth
- Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR)
- User growth rate
- Revenue growth rate
- Market share growth

### Operational Metrics

**For SaaS:**
- Monthly Active Users (MAU)
- Activation rate
- Feature adoption rate
- Time to value
- API usage metrics

**For E-commerce/Physical Products:**
- Inventory turnover
- Average Order Value (AOV)
- Cart abandonment rate
- Return rate
- Fulfillment time

### Team Metrics

- Team size
- Burn rate per employee
- Revenue per employee
- Hiring velocity
- Employee retention

## Benchmarking

### Industry Comparisons

- Compare metrics to industry standards
- Identify if metrics are "good" or need work
- Set realistic targets
- Track competitive positioning

### Stage-Appropriate Metrics

Different metrics matter at different stages:
- **Pre-product:** Focus on validation metrics
- **MVP:** Focus on engagement and feedback
- **Early traction:** Focus on growth and retention
- **Scaling:** Focus on unit economics and efficiency
- **Mature:** Focus on profitability and market position

## Notes

This metrics system should:
- Make it impossible for entrepreneurs to be caught off-guard by investor questions
- Provide confidence through deep understanding of their numbers
- Guide focus on metrics that actually matter
- Help tell a compelling, data-driven story
- Reduce time spent on reporting and increase time on building

The system acts as a CFO + investor relations advisor, making funding-readiness accessible to all entrepreneurs.

# Business Stage System

## Core Concept

A **stage-gate methodology** that ensures entrepreneurs build quality businesses through systematic progression.

## Stage Progression Rules

### Confidence Threshold
- Minimum **8/10 confidence level** required to advance
- Both user AND agents must agree on readiness
- Prevents rushing through important foundational work

### Quality Assurance
- Ensures users build **quality businesses**
- Prevents creation of "slop or junk" businesses
- Forces proper validation and planning at each stage

## Proposed Business Stages

### 1. Ideation
**Focus:** Concept development and initial validation

**Key Activities:**
- Problem identification
- Solution brainstorming
- Initial market hypothesis
- Value proposition development

**Exit Criteria:**
- Clear problem statement
- Potential solution defined
- Target customer identified
- Confidence: 8/10

---

### 2. Research
**Focus:** Market and competitive analysis

**Key Activities:**
- Market size estimation
- Competitor analysis
- Customer research and interviews
- Trend analysis
- Regulatory/compliance research

**Exit Criteria:**
- Market opportunity validated
- Competitors mapped
- Customer insights gathered
- Confidence: 8/10

---

### 3. Validation
**Focus:** Problem-solution fit validation

**Key Activities:**
- Customer interviews and surveys
- Landing page tests
- Problem validation
- Willingness to pay assessment
- Early adopter identification

**Exit Criteria:**
- Problem validated with real customers
- Solution approach confirmed
- Early interest demonstrated
- Confidence: 8/10

---

### 4. MVP Planning
**Focus:** Minimum viable product specification

**Key Activities:**
- Feature prioritization
- MVP scope definition
- Technical architecture planning
- Resource planning
- Timeline estimation

**Exit Criteria:**
- Clear MVP specification
- Technical approach defined
- Resource needs identified
- Confidence: 8/10

---

### 5. MVP Development
**Focus:** Building the initial product

**Key Activities:**
- Core feature development
- Basic UI/UX implementation
- Testing and bug fixing
- Documentation
- Infrastructure setup

**Exit Criteria:**
- MVP functional and stable
- Core value proposition delivered
- Ready for user testing
- Confidence: 8/10

---

### 6. Testing
**Focus:** User testing and iteration

**Key Activities:**
- Beta user recruitment
- User testing sessions
- Feedback collection and analysis
- Iteration and improvements
- Metrics tracking

**Exit Criteria:**
- User feedback incorporated
- Product-market fit signals
- Key metrics trending positively
- Confidence: 8/10

---

### 7. Launch Preparation
**Focus:** Go-to-market planning

**Key Activities:**
- Marketing strategy development
- Sales process design
- Pricing finalization
- Launch materials creation
- Support systems setup

**Exit Criteria:**
- GTM strategy complete
- Launch materials ready
- Support infrastructure in place
- Confidence: 8/10

---

### 8. Launch
**Focus:** Product launch and initial traction

**Key Activities:**
- Public launch execution
- Marketing campaigns
- Customer acquisition
- Performance monitoring
- Rapid iteration based on feedback

**Exit Criteria:**
- Product publicly available
- Initial customers acquired
- Feedback loop established
- Confidence: 8/10

---

### 9. Growth
**Focus:** Scaling and optimization

**Key Activities:**
- Customer acquisition scaling
- Revenue growth
- Team building (if applicable)
- Process optimization
- Market expansion

**Exit Criteria:**
- Sustainable growth trajectory
- Repeatable processes
- Strong unit economics
- Confidence: 8/10

---

### 10. Maturity
**Focus:** Established business operations

**Key Activities:**
- Operational excellence
- Market leadership
- Innovation and new products
- Team and culture building
- Strategic partnerships

**Exit Criteria:**
- Stable, profitable operations
- Market position established
- Ongoing innovation
- Confidence: 8/10

---

## Stage Management Features

### Visual Progress Indicator
- Show current stage
- Display progress within stage
- Show confidence level meter
- Highlight blockers or gaps

### Stage Checklist
- Each stage has completion checklist
- Track completed vs. pending items
- Agent and human task assignment
- Real-time progress updates

### Confidence Assessment
- Regular confidence checks (daily/weekly)
- Both user and agent input
- Identify gaps reducing confidence
- Actionable recommendations to improve

### Stage Lock Mechanism
- Cannot skip stages
- Cannot advance without 8/10 confidence
- Can always go back to previous stages to refine
- Override capability for edge cases (with warnings)

## Notes

This stage framework is inspired by:
- Lean Startup methodology
- Design Thinking process
- Traditional stage-gate product development
- Y Combinator startup guidance

The exact stages and criteria should be refined based on:
- User feedback
- Common startup patterns
- What leads to successful businesses
- Different business types (SaaS vs. physical products, etc.)

## Credit System Explained

### What Are Credits?

Credits are the currency used to power AI interactions in business-os.

**How Credits Work:**
- Each AI model call consumes credits
- Different models cost different amounts
- Different task complexities cost different amounts
- Credits refresh monthly with subscription

### Credit Costs (Example Pricing)

**By Model:**
- **Light Model** (Quick tasks): 1-2 credits
- **Medium Model** (Standard tasks): 5-10 credits
- **Heavy Model** (Complex analysis): 20-50 credits
- **Claude Code Session**: 50 credits/hour

**By Task Type:**
- Simple chat message: 1-2 credits
- Business analysis: 10-20 credits
- Financial modeling: 30-50 credits
- Technical coding (via Claude Code): 50+ credits
- Pitch deck review: 20-30 credits

### Credit Management

**For Users:**
- Dashboard shows credit usage
- Alerts when running low
- Option to purchase additional credits
- Rollover policy (TBD)

**Overage Options:**
- **Option 1:** Pay-as-you-go ($X per 100 credits)
- **Option 2:** Upgrade to higher tier
- **Option 3:** Wait for monthly refresh

**Smart Usage:**
- System recommends optimal model for task
- Users can choose lighter models to save credits
- Usage analytics help optimize spending
